pianola

/pɪəˈnəʊlə/
(noun) A self-playing, self-composing AI piano.

Welcome to Pianola!

Create music by playing a short snippet or choosing an example seed from the Play menu. Sit back as the piano continues on from your prompt, or play along to influence the direction of the piece!

Tip: Want a different continuation? Rewind (left double tap) and have the AI try again!
